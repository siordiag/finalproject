The Best Grilled Cheese Sandwich

December 5, 2024. By Gabrielle Siordia

10 Minutes / Serves 1

Adopted from Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe by Love and Lemons

A classic comfort recipe you'll want to make over and over again

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • Mayonnaise or butter
  • 1 to 2 ounces grated sharp cheddar cheese, or cheese of choice
  • Butter, for the pan (if using mayonnaise)

Directions

  1. Place the bread slices on a cutting board and spread the top side with a thin layer of mayo. Place your cheese in the middle of the bread slices.
  2. Heat a nonstick or cast-iron skillet over medium-low heat and melt enough butter in the bottom of the pan to coat it. Place the sandwich in the pan, cover, and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and crisp. Flip, replace the lid, and cook until the other slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, 1 to 3 minutes. Reduce the heat to low if the bread is getting too brown before the cheese is fully melted.
  3. Lightly squeeze the lemon wedge or juice into the avocado and sprinkle with sea salt
  4. Slice in half and serve alone or with a side of tomato soup.
