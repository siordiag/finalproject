4 Air Fryer Sweet Potato Fries

December 5, 2024. By Gabrielle Siordia

30 Minutes / Serves 2

Adopted from Air Fryer Sweet Potato Fries by Budget Bytes

These sweet potato fries are the best healthy snack - no oven required!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Seasoning of your choice

Directions

  1. Wash, peel, and dry the sweet potatoes. Cut the sweet potatoes into wedges.
  2. Place the sweet potato wedges in a large bowl, drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle seasoning over the top. Toss the sweet potatoes until they're evenly covered in oil and seasoning.
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 400ÂºF. Once preheated, add the seasoned sweet potatoes and spread them as evenly as possible inside the air fryer basket. Cook the fries at five-minute intervals, shaking the basket to flip and stir the fries after each interval.
  4. Continue cooking until the sweet potatoes are slightly blistered and browned on the edges, about 15-20 minutes total. Serve immediately.
