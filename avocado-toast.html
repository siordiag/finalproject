Easy Avocado Toast

December 5, 2024. By Gabrielle Siordia

5 Minutes / Serves 2

Adopted from Avocado Toast by Love and Lemons

The easiest and filling breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Lemon wedge or lemon juice
  • Sea salt
  • 2 to 4 slices of bread, toasted (sourdough is best)

Directions

  1. Halve the avocado vertically and remove the pit. Use a small knife to dice the avocado flesh while it's still inside the skin.
  2. Scoop the diced avocado flesh out of the skin and into a small bowl
  3. Lightly squeeze the lemon wedge or juice into the avocado and sprinkle with sea salt
  4. Mash the diced avocado with a fork. Scoop onto the bread and enjoy!
